Chicken Burger

The whole family will enjoys these burgers, even fussy children. The onion can be chopped up very fine or even grate it to be hidden completely. This quick andeasy too with prep time being 10 mins and cook time only 10 mins too.

Ingridients

  • 1 pound extra-lean ground chicken
  • ½ small onion, finely grated
  • ½ cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs, divided
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine ground chicken, onion, ¼ cup bread crumbs, egg, and garlic in a bowl; season with salt and black pepper.
  3. Moisten hands; shape chicken mixture into 4 flat, oval-shaped patties.
  4. Spread remaining ¼ cup bread crumbs in a shallow dish; dip patties in bread crumbs to coat.
  5. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Add patties; cook until golden and well browned on the bottoms, 5 to 6 minutes.
  7. Flip patties; cook until other sides are browned, 3 to 4 minutes more.
  8. Serve and enjoy with coleslaw and chips. This make 4 burgers.
